Cabbage

The cabbage are grown and stored at the Farmlands cooperative. Transports to consumers are done via the hub system. After consumtion the remains are composted and returned.

Main inputs: Cabbage seeds, Compost

Social

Farmlands is loved by it´s many visitors, especially children.

Consumer apriciate the quality.

The tradtional back braking weed picking is still common.

Tractor pulled weed picking assistants has been emploied to spare peoples backs.

 

Ecological

Our cabbage is ecologically grown in without chems. A hybrid electric tractor running on photovoltaics and hazelnut oil is used at different stages.
