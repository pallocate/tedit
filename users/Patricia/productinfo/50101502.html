Potatoes

The potatoes are grown and stored at the Farmlands cooperative. Transports to consumers are done via the hub system. After consumtion the remains are composted and returned.

Main inputs: Seed potatoes, Compost

Social

Farmlands is loved by it´s many visitors, especially children.

A relative labour sparing crop. Consumer apriciate the quality.

 

Ecological

A hybrid electric tractor running on photovoltaics and hazelnut oil is used at different stages.

The potatoes are ecologically grown in without chems
