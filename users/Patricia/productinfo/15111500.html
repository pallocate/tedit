Wood gas

Main inputs: ,

The gas is still needed to run the heavy trucks and busses.

Social

The gas is toxic due to it´s content of carbon monoxide.

The regular testing of gas alarms and evacuation drills are not yet up to standards.

 

Ecological

A large amount of wood(and land to grow the wood) is needed for the production.

Large plantations of fast growing Salix has been set up in gas plants vicinity, to replace most of the timber quality wood that is used today.
