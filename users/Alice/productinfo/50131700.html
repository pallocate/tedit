Milk


 

Description:

The milk is produced in respect of cows natural behavior and needs. They spend most of the year gracing outdoors.


   

Production:

Automation is applied whenever possible to lessen menial, heavy and otherwise risky work.


   

Consumption:

Milk is concidered a staple product and most households needs it in one form or another. Some consumers has expressed that they want it packaged in reusable glass bottles instead.
