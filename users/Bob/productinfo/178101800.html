Formosa Heights Taxi


Description:

Transports people and cargo in the Formosa heights area. One electric car is used mainly for personal transportation. One large diesel van mainly for cargo.


   

Production:

Sometimes the workdays can be long and hectic, sometimes onerous and dull.


   

Consumption:

There have been some complaints that one electric car is not nearly enough, manytimes the waiting time can be long, especially at large events/weekends or when the car is being charged
